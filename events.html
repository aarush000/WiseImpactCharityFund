Eric Zhang
Eric Zhang is a Principal at Patient Square Capital, a dedicated healthcare investment firm. He has spent a decade in healthcare finance and investing, starting his career in the Investment Banking Division at Goldman Sachs, and later joining TPG Capital’s private equity team. Eric holds an MBA from Stanford, where he was an Arjay Miller Scholar, and a BA, summa cum laude, from Northwestern University.

Lily Huang is a healthcare-focused professional currently serving on the boards of Curana, Elion, and Vori Health. She's also a board observer for Aetion, Belong Health, Everside Health, Strive Health, and TriValence. Before joining NEA, she worked at TPG Capital's North America Buyouts Group, concentrating on healthcare investments.
